## What The Section Should Do

- Re-state the paper's narrow forecasting-to-decision evaluation claim.
- Keep the implementation-side reading clear.
- End with a short, credible forward-looking sentence rather than a long future-work list.

## What To Emphasize

- The paper separates forecast quality from realized decision quality under frictions.
- The evidence comes from a narrow case study with fixed predictive information.
- The main result is still the RL selected-point comparison, with accounting used to justify the evaluation object.

## What To Compress

- Dense-friction provenance details should move to appendix or internal documentation.
- CC-TA-LBIP robustness detail should be mentioned only briefly as auxiliary support.

## Tone Guardrails

- Avoid repeated `we do not claim ...` lists.
- Prefer affirmative framing such as `the paper studies` and `the evidence supports`.
- Keep limitations to about four compact points rather than a long inventory of omitted work.
- Do not let rebuild history or audit language dominate the section.

## Recommended Limitation Set

1. Narrow portfolio-domain case study, not a universal theorem.
2. Fixed 27-name snapshot and canonical frozen split, not a broad universe study.
3. Frozen-policy comparison, not an end-to-end retraining study.
4. Dense-friction, comparator, and same-forecast materials remain support with appropriately limited interpretation.
